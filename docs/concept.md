# potmagic: The Digital Puppet Theater

> **potmagic** is a collaborative storytelling platform designed for families, friends, and creators to perform live, interactive shows without physical constraints. It captures the magic of traditional shadow puppetry and reinvents it for the digital age.

---

## 1. The Vision

In a world of passive consumption, **potmagic** returns to the roots of active storytelling. It is built for:

- **The Director:** Someone with a story to tell but no physical stage.
- **The Actors:** Those looking to share a moment of connection across any distance.

---

## 2. The Director’s Workshop

The Director is the architect of the experience. Here is the step-by-step setup for a production like _Little Red Riding Hood_:

### **Step 1: Script & Scene Mapping**

- **Narrative Flow:** Input story beats and define settings (e.g., The Village, The Deep Bavarian Forest, Grandma’s Cottage).
- **Atmosphere:** Select **Background Layers** (parallax scrolling art) and **Music Loops** (e.g., "Whimsical Forest" or "Suspenseful Growl").

### **Step 2: Asset Curation (Props & Puppets)**

- **Character Assignment:** Select "Digital Puppets"—2D or 3D assets that respond to actor input.
- **Virtual Props:** Add interactive elements like a "Basket of Treats" or a "Hidden Wolf" behind a tree.

### **Step 3: Casting & Invitations**

- **Role Assignment:** Assign characters to specific people (e.g., Daughter as "Red," Grandpa as "The Wolf").
- **The Call:** Send unique secure links containing a **Digital Mask**—the specific puppet that actor will control.

### **Step 4: The Rehearsal (The Sandbox)**

- The stage opens in **Draft Mode** for actors to test movements via mouse, touch, or motion-tracking.
- The Director uses live voice-chat to give notes and coaching.

---

## 3. The Actor’s Stage

Being an actor on potmagic is about performance and spontaneity.

- **Entry:** Click the invitation link to enter the **Backstage View**.
- **Control:** Maneuver your character’s silhouette or stylized puppet on screen.
- **Voice:** Transmit live audio with optional **Character Filters** (e.g., deepening the Wolf’s voice).
- **Interaction:** View **Audience Sparks** (emojis/text) in the corner of the screen to react to viewers in real-time.

---

## 4. Showtime: The Live Broadcast

- **The Link:** A public or private **Curtain Link** is shared with the audience.
- **Live Engagement:** Viewers act as the "Village," triggering sound effects (applause, gasps) or voting on plot points (e.g., "Should Red take the shortcut?").
- **Emotional Connection:** The Director orchestrates transitions live while actors provide the soul of the performance.
